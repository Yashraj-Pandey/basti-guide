Did You Know? 🎓

The Name Origin

Basti was originally known as Vaishishthi because it was the site of Rishi Vashistha's Ashram in ancient times.

Revolutionary History

The Chhawani area in Basti is a major memorial of the 1857 revolution, where hundreds of freedom fighters were martyred.

Literary Greats

Basti is the birthplace of the famous Hindi critic Acharya Ram Chandra Shukla, whose house is still a historical landmark.
